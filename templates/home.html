Simple Flow

From idea to answer in seconds

Nexus AI is designed to be zero-friction. No accounts, no setup — just open and go.

01

Choose Your Mode

Select one of three AI modes from the sidebar: Answer Questions, Summarize Text, or Creative Content. Each mode is optimized for a different type of task.

Q&A — Best for facts, research, coding, and explanations
Summarize — Best for long documents, articles, and emails
Creative — Best for writing, brainstorming, and storytelling
02

Pick a Prompt Style

Fine-tune how Nexus AI responds by selecting a Prompt Style from the sidebar. Each mode offers multiple styles that change the tone, format, and depth of the response.

Concise — Short, direct answers with no fluff
Detailed — In-depth explanations with examples and context
Socratic / Casual — Guided or conversational tone
03

Type Your Prompt

Enter your question, paste a block of text, or describe what you want to create. Use the text box at the bottom — hit Enter or click the send button.

Tip: Be specific — "Explain photosynthesis for a 10-year-old" beats just "photosynthesis"
Follow-ups work! Ask "Can you simplify that?" or "Give me an example"
04

Get Your Answer — Instantly

Nexus routes your request through its Smart LLM Router, which automatically picks the fastest available AI provider. If one hits a rate limit, it silently falls back to the next — you always get a response.

Auto-failover — Gemini → Groq → Cohere → HuggingFace
Rate each response — Your feedback improves future results
Markdown rendered — Code, lists, and bold text display beautifully
